Key Points of BRSSI

Study Material

Covering each and every intricate concept along with proper illustrations. Classroom Discussion Problem, On-line Tests and Home Assignments.

24 x 7 Doubt Clearing

The main objective of doubt clearing class/session is to provide additional attention and support (by BRSSI) to the students. It generally helps those students who couldn't understand a concept at the beginning. It is a One to One Discussion Forum between the student and the teacher.

Performance Analysis

The academic performance of a student is analyzed and studied at Micro level (i.e. subject wise, topic wise and question wise) and special academic initiatives are taken to improve the performance

About Us


Barrackpore R.S Success Institute (BRSSI) came into existence in the year 2010 on 20th March. This institute was founded by SMT. RINA SAHA. At first it started functioning from Latbagan and after 2011 it shifted to its new address near Barrackpore Municipality. In our institute we basically offer private coaching session to students for Foundation course (Class I to Class XII) as well as Engineering course (Class XI to Class XII) also. We prepare students in a scientific way for different examinations of Schools, Institutes & Boards (West Bengal, CBSE, ICSE). BRSSI wished to impart holistic education to the knowledge hungry brains of India for their academic excellence. We continually respond to the upcoming changes in the field of education and incorporate them to the best of our abilities. We strive to reach out to perfection through a diligent academic pursuit for excellence. We further envision ourselves as a valued and honored institute engaged in equipping students with knowledge and helping them realize their potentialities.
