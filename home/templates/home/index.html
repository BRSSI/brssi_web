About Us


Barrackpore R.S Success Institute (BRSSI) came into existence in the year 2010 on 20th March. This institute was founded by SMT. RINA SAHA. At first it started functioning from Latbagan and after 2011 it shifted to its new address near Barrackpore Municipality. In our institute we basically offer private coaching session to students for Foundation course (Class I to Class XII) as well as Engineering course (Class XI to Class XII) also. We prepare students in a scientific way for different examinations of Schools, Institutes & Boards (West Bengal, CBSE, ICSE).
